Frog Trap Game

How to play: Trap the frog on one lily pad by clicking on the surrounding lily pads. One click of a lily pad, the frog moves once, too. Don't let him reach the outermost pads, or else he'll escape the pond!


Tip: Make use of the sunken lily pads and create an enclosed area trapping the frog.
