Mechanics


1. The game starts with the frog at the center and a random lily pad board generated. The game's objective is to keep the frog trapped on the pond on one lily pad.

2. When you click on a lily pad, the frog moves once to any lily pads beside him. You is not allowed to click again on the sunken lily pads.


3. You can think of a plan on how to trap the frog and maybe predict the frog's moves. For instance, this is a possible way:

blue represents the order of clicking and red represents the order of the frog's moves

4. You can always come up with a different strategy to trap the frog.
  - Start clicking far from the frog
  - Establish corners of the trap then filling it in
  - Pray for a good lily pad board to generate
  - Wait for your luck to come
