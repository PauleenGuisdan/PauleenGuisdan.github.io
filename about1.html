About the Game

The Frog Trap Game has only one simple objective. Keep the frog trapped in the pond! Do not let the frog get to the outermost lily pads, or else he’ll escape!

This game trains and sharpens your critical thinking skills. It enhances and trains your brain to solve problems better (we hope T-T). With each click of a lily pad, the frog moves once, as well. Do not let the frog play tricks on you, you can do it! Outsmart him! It’s okay if you fail… you can try again by clicking the reset button! The lily pad board randomizes everytime a new game starts or when the reset button is clicked.

This game is inspired by the Cat Trap Game, whose objective and mechanics are the same, but it’s a cat.

It’s addicting! You’ll feel rewarded once you get the hang of it. Have fun playing! Don’t forget to also check out our developers above. Thank you! ❤
